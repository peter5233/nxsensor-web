Why Module-Level Monitoring?

A challenge of improving solar power system is the lack of understanding of
how PV modules deteriorate over time and the lack of knowing their impairments.

Great Granularity

String monitoring systems lack accuracy. Module faults having built up over time may go undetected and cause losses.

Smart Maintenance

O&M teams are able to find failed panels with certainty. They can remotely diagnose problems and determine remedy.

Better Returns

NxSensor detects hidden failures, enables immediate O&M actions, thus avoiding losses and achieving better yields.

Enables Advanced Solar Energy

By integrating smart electronics (MLPE) in junction box, NxSensor brings more functionalities to solar power systems.

Module Monitoring

Monitors module's voltage and temperature, digitizing system performance at module level.

Rapid Shutdown

Complies with 2017 NEC 690.12 rapid shutdown requirements, ensuring safety of the operators.

Reliability & Safety

Identifies erroneous electrical connection in the junction box, improving reliability & safety.

Wireless Mesh Network

Data acquisition & communication using cutting-edge wireless mesh network technology - HOHWave™.

HOHWave™ is the best solution for module-level
