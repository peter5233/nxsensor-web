Why Module-Level Monitoring?

A challenge of improving solar power system is the lack of understanding of
how PV modules deteriorate over time and the lack of knowing their impairments.

Great Granularity

String monitoring systems lack accuracy. Module faults that have built up over time may easily go undetected.

Efficient Maintenance

O&M teams are able to find failed panels with certainty. They can remotely identify problems and determine remedy.

Better Financial Returns

NxSensor detects hidden failures, enables immediate O&M actions, thus avoiding losses and achieving better yields.

Enables Smarter Solar Energy

By integrating smart electronics in the junction box, NxSensor brings more functionalities to solar modules.

Module Monitoring

Monitors module's voltage and temperature, digitizing module-level performance.

Rapid Shutdown

Complies with 2017 NEC 690.12 rapid shutdown requirements, ensuring the safety of operators.

Reliability & Safety

Identifies erroneous electrical connection in the junction box, improving reliability & safety.

Wireless Mesh Network

Sends data to a gateway using cutting-edge wireless mesh network technology - HOHWave™.
