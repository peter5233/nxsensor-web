Why Module-Level Monitoring?

A challenge of improving solar power system is the lack of understanding of
how PV modules deteriorate over time and the lack of knowing their impairments.

Great Granularity

String and inverter monitoring systems lack accuracy. Module faults that have built up over time may easily go undetected or unreported.

Efficient Maintenance

O&M teams are able to find failed panels with certainty. They can remotely identify problems and determine remedy before arriving on-site.

Better Financial Returns

NxSensor system detects hidden failures of PV modules, enables immediate O&M action, thus avoiding losses and achieving better yields.
